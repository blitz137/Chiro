ABOUT US

Smith Chiropractic exists to heal from the inside out. We understand that when the body isn’t functioning as it was designed to, the whole system gets buggy.

We service a wide variety of patient needs by assessing and correcting mechanical dysfunctions.

However, we specialize in correcting cervical and lumbar (sciatica) disc disorders and identify and correct athletic injuries.

Our goal is to send every patient home with a hug. A very big hug. We believe that happiness heals.
